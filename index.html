WOK - World of Kitchens

World of Kitchens (WOK) is a game designed in a culinary metaverse that provides the possibility of farming and the collection of NFTs that can be used in a strategy game (RPG) based on the operation of a restaurant in different spaces and the control of resources adapted to the culinary theme.
